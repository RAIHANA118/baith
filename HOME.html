Our Products

NATURAL BBQ CHARCOL

Natural BBQ charcoal, often referred to as lump charcoal, is a popular choice among grilling enthusiasts for its purity and performance.Being free from chemicals, natural lump charcoal imparts a subtle, smoky flavor to food, enhancing its natural taste.

SAWDUST BBQ CHARCOL

Sawdust charcoal, also known as sawdust briquette charcoal, is an eco-friendly and efficient fuel made by compressing and carbonizing sawdust a byproduct of wood processing. This type of charcoal is popular for grilling and barbecuing due to its long burn time, high heat output, and low smoke production.

COCONUT SHISHA CHARCOL

Coconut shell charcoal is a popular choice for shisha (hookah) enthusiasts due to its eco-friendliness, clean burn, and minimal ash production. Made from compressed coconut shells, these charcoals are known for their long lasting heat and odorless combustion, enhancing the flavor of your shisha sessions.
